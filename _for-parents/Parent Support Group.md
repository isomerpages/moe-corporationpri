<h4>Shared Values</h4>
<p>Lead, Care, Inspire</p>
<h4>Mission Statement</h4>
<p>The early years of childhood are fundamental in shaping a child's future,
making positive guidance during this crucial period essential for fostering
well-rounded and responsible individuals. Our mission is to actively contribute
to this development by working closely with teachers at Corporation Primary
School, supporting students in refining their talents and reaching their
full potential.</p>
<h4>Join our Parent Support Group Family</h4>
<p>We invite you to consider joining our Parent Support Group (PSG). If your
child or ward is currently a student of Corporation Primary School, and
you have a keen interest in volunteering within our school community, kindly
submit your PSG application through the online form by scanning the QR
code below.</p>
