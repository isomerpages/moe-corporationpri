Financial Assistance
====================

  

---

**Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)**  

Singapore citizens in financial need can receive assistance from MOE with school fees and other expenses.
Please visit https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance for more information.
