<h1>ADMISSION &amp; SCHOOL TRANSFER</h1>
<h3><strong>2026 P1 Registration Exercise</strong></h3>
<hr>
<p>Phase 1 registration is for a Singapore Citizen (SC) or Permanent Resident
(PR) child born between 2 January 2020 and 1 January 2021 (both dates inclusive)
and has a sibling currently studying in our school.</p>
<p>If your child is SC / PR:</p>
<p>Parents who are registering their younger child under Phase 1 are to register<strong> from 9am on Tuesday, 30 June 2026 to 4.30pm on Thursday, 2 July 2026</strong>.</p>
<p>If your child is a non-SC / non-PR (not eligible for Phase 1):</p>
<p>Please submit an online Indication of Interest<strong> from 9am on Tuesday, 19 May 2026 to 4.30pm on Monday, 25 May 2026</strong>,
otherwise you will not be able to register in Phase 3 of the P1 Registration
Exercise. Parents will be notified through email by the end of October
on whether their child has been offered a place in a primary school.</p>
<p>For the latest updates on P1 Registration Exercise, please visit <a href="https://go.gov.sg/P1reg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/P1reg</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Leave of Absence (LOA) Scheme</strong>
</p>
<p>The LOA scheme is for current CPS pupils who fulfil the following criteria:</p>
<p>• Pupil is a Singapore Citizen or PR</p>
<p>• Pupil is accompanying parent on an overseas posting</p>
<p>To apply: Email the school at <a href="mailto:cps@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">cps@moe.edu.sg</a> and our school staff will
be in touch with you.</p>
<p>For more information: <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/returning-singaporeans" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/returning-singaporeans</a>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Compulsory Education</strong>
</p>
<p>All Singapore Citizens born after 1 January 1996 and living in Singapore
must attend a national primary school unless an exemption is granted.</p>
<p>For more information: <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/compulsory-education" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/compulsory-education</a>
