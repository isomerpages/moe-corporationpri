<h1>ICT</h1>
<p><strong>Our Vision:</strong>&nbsp;Nurturing Responsible Digital Learners,
Empowering Teachers as Digital Curators.</p>
<p><strong>Our Mission:</strong>&nbsp;To create an ICT learning environment
which inspires students and staff to deepen learning and develop 21st Century
Competencies.</p>
<p>The school ensures students exhibit adequate level of self-directed and
collaborative skills. We develop programmes that uncover the joy of learning
among our students using technology. The school will equip students with
life-long social-emotional competencies and sound values so that they can
become safe, respectful and responsible users of ICT.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Our Student-Focused ICT Approach</strong>: At Corporation Primary
School, we harness technology to amplify learning, personalise progress,
and foster creativity. In line with the <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/education-in-sg/educational-technology-journey/edtech-masterplan" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">MOE EdTech Masterplan 2030</a>,
our programmes ensure students:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Take ownership of their learning through personalised tools such as the
<a href="https://vle.learning.moe.edu.sg/login" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Student Learning Space</a>(SLS) and Adaptive Learning Systems.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate meaningfully using digital platforms to build and share ideas
across time and space.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Think critically and safely when using technology, practising good cyber
wellness habits, and upholding academic integrity in the age of Artificial
Intelligence (AI).</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop digital and social-emotional competencies so they become safe,
respectful, and responsible users of ICT.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>2026 Focus Areas</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="https://vle.learning.moe.edu.sg/login" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Student Learning Space</a> (SLS)
– Using its interactive, multimodal features to differentiate learning
and provide targeted feedback.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital Literacy &amp; Technological Skills (DLTS) – Strengthening information
skills, metacognition, and responsible online behaviour.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Safe &amp; Ethical Use of AI – Teaching students to critically evaluate
AI outputs, cite AI-assisted work, and protect personal data.</p>
</li>
</ol>
